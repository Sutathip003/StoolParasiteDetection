Dataset references for stool parasite detection.

1. Low-Cost Microscopic Images for Parasitic Egg Detection
   Source: Zenodo
   URL: https://zenodo.org/records/10437395
   License: CC BY 4.0
   Notes: Open access dataset of stool parasite egg images captured by microscope.

2. ParasitoBank: Dataset of helminths and protozoa in coprology samples
   Source: Mendeley Data
   URL: https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/725hpzpwzf/1
   License: CC BY 4.0
   Notes: COCO-style annotations for microscope fecal images.
